# Task 019, Card Selection UI Test

## Goal

Test Grave Goblin card selection behaviour.

## Steps

1. Press Play.
2. Wait for energy to reach 2.
3. Tap Grave Goblin card.
4. Confirm card lifts and glows.
5. Confirm deployment area appears.
6. Tap valid arena point.
7. Confirm goblin spawns.
8. Confirm card deselects.

## Done when

Card selection feels clear and tactile.
