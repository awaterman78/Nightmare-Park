# Monster Role System v1

## Roles
Pressure, cheap fast units that force a response.
Ranged, fragile damage from distance.
Swarm, multiple small units.
Tank, high health, absorbs fire.
Control, stun, slow or disrupt.
Assassin, fast unit that punishes backline.
Support, heals or buffs.
Bruiser, strong all-round threat.
Spell, instant effect with no walking body.

## Rarity rules
Common teaches core mechanics.
Rare introduces sharper counters.
Epic adds bigger personality and stronger effects.
Legendary must feel memorable and emotionally exciting.

## Balance rule
Every monster needs something it beats and something that beats it.
No card should be universally correct.
