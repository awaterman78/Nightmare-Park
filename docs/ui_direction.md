# UI Direction

## Target feel

Premium haunted mobile battler.

Not cluttered. Not prototypey. Not spreadsheet-like.

## Main battle screen

Top:

- Enemy core health.
- Timer.
- Your core health.

Small stats:

- Screams.
- Bones.
- Chaos.

Middle:

- Arena One, full focus.
- Units and towers clearly visible.
- No big tutorial box over the action.

Bottom:

- Energy orb and segmented energy bar.
- Card hand.
- Grave Goblin card in first test.
- Empty card slots are acceptable during prototype.

## UI rules

- Arena is the star.
- No giant instruction panel over the battlefield.
- Avoid ugly placeholder icons.
- Use gothic panels and clean readable numbers.
- Energy and cards should feel tactile.
- Card selection should visibly lift/glow.
- Deployment area should have subtle feedback only.
