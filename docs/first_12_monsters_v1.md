# First 12 Monsters v1

## Grave Goblin
Common, cost 2, fast melee pressure, Quick Scuttle, Bone Stab. First attack deals bonus damage. Build priority 1.

## Candle Imp
Common, cost 2, cheap ranged attacker, Hopping Dash, Fire Spit. Light splash on impact. Build priority 2.

## Tin Bat
Common, cost 2, flying swarm, Flutter Swarm, Dive Peck. Deploys as three small flying units. Build priority 4.

## Coffin Guard
Common, cost 3, mini tank, Heavy Shuffle, Lid Block. Brief damage reduction after deployment. Build priority 5.

## Bone Archer
Common, cost 3, ranged single-target, Cautious Step, Bone Shot. Reliable ranged anti-air damage. Build priority 3.

## Zombie Runner
Rare, cost 3, fast assassin, Sprint Lunge, Infected Bite. First bite applies damage over time. Build priority 6.

## Mirror Clown
Rare, cost 4, control, Jittery Sidestep, Mirror Stun. Periodically stuns nearest enemy. Build priority 7.

## Venom Widow
Rare, cost 4, poison control, Wall-Creep Skitter, Venom Bite. Applies poison over time. Build priority 10.

## Bone Brute
Epic, cost 5, tank, Heavy Stomp, Skull Smash. Heavy attack damages and knocks back nearby enemies. Build priority 8.

## Phantom Bride
Epic, cost 5, support, Ghost Glide, Healing Wail. Pulses healing to nearby allies. Build priority 9.

## Werewolf King
Legendary, cost 6, bruiser, Savage Charge, Moon Rage. Gains speed and attack rate after taking damage. Build priority 12.

## Cursed Lightning
Epic, cost 4, spell, Spell Strike, Chain Zap. Chains to nearby enemies with reduced damage. Build priority 11.
