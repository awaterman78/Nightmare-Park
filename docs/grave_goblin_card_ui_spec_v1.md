Skill:

```text
Bone Stab
```

## Card states

### Normal

Card sits in bottom hand.

Cost badge visible.

Name visible.

Portrait visible.

### Affordable

When energy is 2 or higher:

Card appears bright and playable.

### Not affordable

When energy is below 2:

Card appears dimmed.

Cost badge remains visible.

### Selected

Card lifts upward.

Green glow appears.

Deployment zone activates.

### Cooldown

Not required for first test.

## Card layout

Top left:

Energy cost orb.

Main area:

Grave Goblin portrait.

Bottom:

Name and small movement label.

Suggested text:

```text
GRAVE GOBLIN
Quick Scuttle
```

## Tap behaviour

Tap card once:

Select.

Tap same card again:

Deselect.

Tap valid arena after selecting:

Deploy and deselect.

## Quality bar

The card should feel tactile.

It should not look like a flat rectangle with text dumped on it.
