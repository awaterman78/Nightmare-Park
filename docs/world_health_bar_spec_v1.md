# World Health Bar Spec v1

## Purpose

Units, towers and cores need small readable health bars.

## Unit health bar

Small bar above unit.

Player colour:

```text
Green
```

Enemy colour:

```text
Red or purple
```

## Tower health bar

Slightly larger than unit health bar.

Should sit above tower.

## Core health

Main core health is shown in top HUD.

World core health bar is optional.

## Behaviour

Health bar appears when damaged or always visible for first test.

For first Unity slice, always visible is acceptable.

## Quality rule

Health bars should be clear but not massive.

They should not make Grave Goblin look like he is wearing a billboard.
