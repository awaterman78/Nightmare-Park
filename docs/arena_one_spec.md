# Arena One Spec

## Name

Arena One, Haunted Graveyard Park

## Purpose

This is the first competitive battlefield and the visual foundation for the whole game.

It must be beautiful, but gameplay readability comes first.

## Layout

- Enemy core at the top.
- Player core at the bottom.
- Two defensive towers per side.
- A cursed green fog river / trench separating the halves.
- Two bridge crossings.
- Player can place monsters anywhere in their half.
- Enemy can place monsters anywhere in their half.
- Units path naturally to the nearest useful bridge or target.

## Visual identity

- Gothic haunted theme park.
- Graveyard stone paths.
- Green magical fog.
- Purple haunted lighting.
- Skull architecture.
- Pumpkins and lanterns.
- Twisted trees.
- Wrought iron fences.
- Haunted castle base structures.

## Gameplay zones

### Player deployment half

Bottom half of the arena. Units can be placed freely here.

### Enemy deployment half

Top half of the arena. Enemy AI places units here.

### No deployment zone

The middle river, bridges and opponent half.

### Battle bridges

Units cross through the left or right bridge depending on placement and target.

## Readability rules

- Do not add lane markings over the art.
- Use very subtle deploy tint only when player is selecting a card.
- Towers and cores need visible health bars.
- Active units need health bars.
- Attack effects must be bright and short.
- The arena should never hide the monsters.
