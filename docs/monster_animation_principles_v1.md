# Monster Animation Principles v1

Every monster must move differently.

A player should recognise a monster by movement alone.

## Movement styles
Quick Scuttle, Grave Goblin, low, twitchy, bursty and uneven.
Hopping Dash, Candle Imp, small hops and flame bounce.
Flutter Swarm, Tin Bat, jittery wing beats and quick dive.
Heavy Shuffle, Coffin Guard, slow dragging mass.
Cautious Step, Bone Archer, rattly skeleton steps.
Sprint Lunge, Zombie Runner, jerky unstable forward surges.
Jittery Sidestep, Mirror Clown, unnatural sideways snaps.
Wall-Creep Skitter, Venom Widow, sharp many-legged movement.
Heavy Stomp, Bone Brute, weight and ground shake.
Ghost Glide, Phantom Bride, floating and cloth trails.
Savage Charge, Werewolf King, powerful clawed rush.
Spell Strike, Cursed Lightning, instant jagged energy.
