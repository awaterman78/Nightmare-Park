# Audio Direction v1

## Overall sound identity

Nightmare Park should sound spooky, sharp and playful.

Not horror film serious.

More:

```text
haunted toy box
graveyard arcade
monster battler
gothic carnival
```

Less:

```text
realistic gore
loud jump scare
muddy dark ambience
generic fantasy sword game
```

## Grave Goblin sound style

Grave Goblin should sound:

- Small
- Scratchy
- Fast
- Nasty
- Slightly funny
- Not cute

## Core Grave Goblin sounds

### Spawn pop

Dirt pop, tiny green puff, goblin arrival.

### Scuttle step

Small scratchy foot taps.

Should be quiet and varied.

### Bone Stab

Bone click plus sharp jab.

First hit can be slightly more powerful.

### Hit squeak

Small angry squeak or grunt.

### Death puff

Tiny yelp, bone rattle, green puff.

## UI sounds

### Card select

Short magical click.

### Invalid placement

Soft dull error blip.

Not annoying.

### Victory

Small haunted victory sting.

### Defeat

Short gloomy sting.

## Audio rule

Sounds must support action without becoming noisy.

Mobile games get annoying fast if common sounds are too loud.
